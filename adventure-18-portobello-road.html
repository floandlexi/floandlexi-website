Fun Facts About Portobello Road Market!

As we rested our tired paws, we learned so many incredible things about this famous market!

  • The Longest Market: Portobello Road Market is nearly 1km long! It features over 1,000 vendors selling a wide range of goods!
  • Antiques Paradise: The Saturday antique market is world-famous, with hundreds of stalls offering unique vintage and antique treasures!
  • Market History: Portobello Road Market dates back to the 1800s, starting as a humble street market for fresh produce and household items!
  • Notting Hill Carnival: The market is at the heart of the annual Notting Hill Carnival, Europe's biggest street festival, held every August bank holiday!
  • Food Lovers' Heaven: A paradise for foodies! The market offers a wide variety of street food from around the world and fresh produce throughout the week!
  • Popular Filming Location: Portobello Road is a famous filming location, most notably for the romantic comedy "Notting Hill" starring Hugh Grant and Julia Roberts!
  • Colorful Houses: The vibrant, pastel-colored houses make Portobello Road one of the most photographed streets in London!
  • Different Market Days: Each day has a different focusâ€”Saturday for antiques, Friday for fashion, and general goods throughout the week!
  • Cultural Melting Pot: The area reflects London's multiculturalism with Caribbean, Portuguese, Spanish, and Moroccan influences!
  • Hidden Gems: Beyond the main market, narrow side streets hide small independent boutiques, galleries, and cafes waiting to be discovered!
